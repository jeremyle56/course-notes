\section{Revision}

Here is a list of concepts you should know from high school.

\begin{itemize}
    \item Vertically opposite, alternating, corresponding and co-interior angles.
    \item Interior angles of a triangle sum to \(\pi\); exterior angle equals sum of opposite interior angles.
    \item Congruent and similar triangles.
    \item Isosceles and equilateral triangles.
    \item Sine rule: \(\frac{a}{\sin \alpha} = \frac{b}{\sin \beta} = \frac{c}{\sin \gamma}\).
    \item Cosine rule: \(a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc \cos \alpha\). Pythagoras' theorem if \(\alpha = \frac{\pi}{2}\).
    \item Three non collinear points defines a unique circle.
    \item A quadrilateral is cyclic if its vertices lie on a circle.
\end{itemize}
